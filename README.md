# Fun Fact about SPIDERMAN
<h3> Who is Spider Man ? </h3>
<body>

<p> Spider-Man is basically your friendly neighborhood superhero who started as an ordinary science-loving teenager named Peter Parker until a radioactive spider bit him and gave him incredible powers like sticking to walls, super strength, lightning-fast reflexes, and a "Spider-Sense" that warns him of danger. Instead of becoming famous or rich, he spends most of his time swinging across New York to stop villains, cracking jokes in the middle of fights, and somehow still stressing about homework, rent, and being late to everything. What really makes Spider-Man special isn't his powers—it's that he chooses to help others no matter how difficult it makes his own life, living by the lesson: "With great power comes great responsibility. </p>
